Welcome to Omelette

What is Omelette

Omelette is a web application that helps cooks build a portfolio of abilities, while allowing restaurant owners and managers find employees faster with the help of a visual/digital resume.

Why have someone tell you they can do something when they can show you they can do it. With Omelette, cooks of all types will be able to upload their abilities onto their Omelette profile to showcase themselves in a visual portfolio.
